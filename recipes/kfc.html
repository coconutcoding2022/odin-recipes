Korean Fried Chicken

Description

This Korean Fried Chicken is a crispy double fried chicken marinated in gochujang sauce. Simple to make, but complex in flavor.

  • chicken
  • vegetable oil
  • salt
  • flour
  • gochujang
  • egg
  • milk
  1. Mix your egg and milk in a bowl.
  2. Coat your chicken in flour and dip it in the bowl we mixed.
  3. Get a pot filled with vegetable oil and throw the chicken in there.
  4. After the chicken is golden brown throw it in the gochujang sauce and salt.
